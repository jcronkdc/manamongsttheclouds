TITLE: King Varas first council meeting
CREATED: 20170329T181652Z
UPDATED: 20220221T192935Z

============================================================

King Veris calls all the leaders from every region for a council meeting. It is in that meeting and he lays down his new laws. He has asked them to bring their most knowledgeable magician so that he may meet with them and discuss the future of magic. He then executes all the magicians and add them to his chamber so that he may drain their power. 

He accepts gives of elderstone, wood magic, blood vine, crystal milk.

He also executes the Lords of each region putting his own men in their place. 

He treats his chamber like a wonder closet. It is his "collection". Although at times he is careless with his use of power and some of them die when he drains too much.  Much like a vampire, except he has a lust for magic instead of blood.
