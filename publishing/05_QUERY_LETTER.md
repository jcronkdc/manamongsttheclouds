# Query Letter — *Man Amongst the Clouds*

## NOTE TO JUSTIN
This query letter is for agents/publishers if you decide to dual-track (query while self-publishing, or query first and self-publish if no bites). If you're going straight to self-pub, skip this — but keep it on file. If the book does well self-published, agents sometimes come to YOU, and having a polished query ready is useful.

**Important:** If you submit to agents, do NOT self-publish first. Most traditional publishers want first publication rights. Pick one track or the other.

---

Dear [Agent Name],

I am seeking representation for *Man Amongst the Clouds*, a literary fantasy novel complete at 153,000 words. It will appeal to readers of Patrick Rothfuss's *The Name of the Wind*, Guy Gavriel Kay's *Tigana*, and Robin Hobb's *Assassin's Apprentice* — epic in scope, intimate in execution, built on a magic system where every act of power costs the user a piece of who they are.

In a world where magic is memory, a boy named Aelo has spent fifteen years in silence — raised by a scarred guardian who fed him herbs each morning to suppress a power that could get them both killed. When the herbs fail, Aelo discovers he can hear the world's memory speak. Every stone, every tree, every drop of rain carries the record of everything it has been. Magic is the act of joining that conversation. And the conversation has a cost: the more you listen, the more of yourself you lose.

A deaf king sits at the center of a dead zone, draining the memories of hundreds to feed a hunger born the day the world chose everyone except him. King Varas cannot hear the Song that underlies all magic. He never could. He has spent seventy years consuming the world to fill a silence nothing can fill — and he has sent his most lethal weapon to find the boy who made a stone sing.

That weapon is a man known only as The Knife, who carries a wooden box of five beautiful objects and checks them every night because the checking is the only proof that a person still exists inside the weapon his father made him. The Knife's memories are fading — consumed by the father who drains him — and the objects are all he has left.

Aelo must cross seven regions, learn five magical disciplines, and confront the king whose deafness has consumed the world. But the prophecy says the Singer who carries all seven voices will "in Singing, cease." To save the world, Aelo may have to dissolve into it.

*Man Amongst the Clouds* is a standalone novel with series potential. It features a magic system rooted in memory and sacrifice, a villain whose monstrousness is fully human, a deuteragonist whose arc from weapon to person is told through five fading objects in a wooden box, and a climax that is a conversation rather than a battle.
