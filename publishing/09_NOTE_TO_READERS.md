# Note to Readers

## A Note from the Author

This book took nine years.

It started in March 2017, in a notes app, in fragments — a scarred man with a staff, a boy who didn't know what he was, a world that remembered everything. I wrote when the compulsion hit. In bursts. In the middle of the night. In texts to myself. In emails with no recipient. I wrote in coffee shops and parking lots and in the silence after my kids fell asleep.

I didn't write it on a schedule. I wrote it because it wouldn't leave me alone.

The man who started this book in 2017 is not the man who finished it in 2026. I'm grateful for that. The years between gave the story a weight I didn't have when I began — a depth that only comes from living, from loss, from learning what it actually costs to carry something all the way to the end.

*Man Amongst the Clouds* is a story about memory, and what we lose to become what we're meant to be. It is dense. It is slow in places. It asks you to sit with sentences the way you'd sit with a friend who needs you to just listen. If you're looking for something fast, this isn't it. If you're looking for something that stays with you — pull up a chair.

Thank you for giving it your time. That's the most valuable thing anyone can offer a story.

— Justin Cronk
