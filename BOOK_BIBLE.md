**Logline:** In a world where magic was once a sacred gift from the gods, a tyrannical king drains the life-force of his people to hoard power — and a gentle, unlikely boy named Nim may be the fulfillment of an ancient prophecy destined to restore the balance.

## II. WORLD HISTORY (The Deep Past)

### The Origin of Magic

Magic was once a right for everyone — a gift from the gods. A guide was given to **King Fletcher** so he could teach magic and bring mankind closer to the gods.

### The Corruption

Many gods were furious. They couldn't erase magic, but they slowly corrupted it with dark magic. Great battles were waged for King Fletcher's scrolls. He destroyed them — and himself — but first stored his power in the **Elder Stone**.

### The Prophecy

> One man of royal blood would discover the Elder Stone's power and restore the balance of magic.

A royal purge followed. The castle was burned. Anyone with royal blood was hunted.

### The Fall

The kings of the Core devised ways to extract magic from people's blood (think: The Matrix pods). Consumed magic was weaker and corrupted the user forever.

## III. THE WORLD — Regions & Peoples

Every region has a wall of its primary elemental magic: **Water, Stone, Fire, Wind, Earth, Metal** — with sub-worlds like **Cloud** and **Tree**.

- **The Pure Ones (Trees):** Samurai-like warriors. Philosophy, honor, observation. Magic revealed at age 12. Some practice the **Rootrun** (trance-running). Have the **Know**.
- **The Core:** Brute strength. Magic of the **Mold** — cities beneath the crust. Weak on the surface but can sink battalions.
- **The Cloud People:** Light, wispy, quick. Dart-like in combat. Carry vials of liquid. Wise, incredible vision. Underestimated.
- **The Sea People:** Slow-moving. Worship sun and moon. Thrive in chaos. Have **Guide magic** — always know their place. Rare few have limited precognition.
- **The Volcano People:** Thick skin (only diamond-tipped arrows pierce). Weakness: cold makes them brittle.

## IV. MAGIC SYSTEM

### Types of Magic

- **The Know** — sense/perceive magic and truth
- **Heal** — healing
- **Mold** — reshape physical matter
- **Move** — telekinesis
- **Guide** — innate direction/precognition
- **Rootrun** — trance endurance
- **Battle Magic** — combat training
- **Blood/Drain Magic** — corrupted extraction from people

### Key Concepts

- **Elder Stone:** Stores immense power. King Fletcher's legacy. Used in the Knower Ceremony.
- **Blood Vine:** Overtakes children during magic testing.
- **Detangling:** Separating partitioned magic within a person. Dangerous — spread too thin and you lose it (or rip your soul out).
- **Magic Suppression Herbs:** Conceal magic; also stunt growth (used by Jalo on Nim).
- **Triddle:** Magical servant (black market item).

### The Knower Ceremony

- **Old way:** Beautiful celebration during the Blue Sun (once yearly, preceded by Blood Moon). Magic freely shared, balance maintained, families given gifts.
- **Under Varas:** Painful, often fatal. Children drained into Elder Stones via machines. Those with promise separated at birth; those without are "invalids" sent to fields. People stopped having children → forced impregnation. One ceremony drained an entire village.
