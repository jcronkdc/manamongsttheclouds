Nine years ago I started writing a book. Today, the first ten chapters are free to read online.

**Man Amongst the Clouds — Part One: The Still Water**

It's a fantasy novel about a world that remembers what people have forgotten. About herbs that heal and herbs that don't. About a boy named Aelo who wakes to smoke and a life he didn't ask for.

No catch. No signup. No account. Just pick a chapter and read.

🔗 https://manamongsttheclouds.com/read/part-one

You can also download it as an EPUB if you'd rather read on your Kindle or phone.

I'm also trying something different — if you feel like it, you can leave feedback at the end of any chapter. I'm crowdsourcing the editing process before final publication. But that's completely optional. The story is here for you either way, whether you want to help shape it or just enjoy the ride.

Part Two (The Waking) is coming May 2026. You can sign up on the page to get notified.

If you know someone who'd enjoy it, there are share buttons right on the page — Facebook, Twitter, email, text, whatever works.
