"Today was the day of the blue sun. As always it was proceeded by the blood moon. Where once there was celebration and salutations, celebrating the powers of the children, and what they may become. Had now become a somber affair. Families sitting quietly at dinner trying not to think about the day to come. During the peak of the blood moon, a century sent by the king would stand guard. Knocking on the door at dawn before the rise of the blue sun and escorting the eligible children to the ceremony square. Any resistance was met with certain death. It had been a long time since anyone resisted. The cost was never worth it.

Some would attempt to block the magic, or drain just enough other children so as to make their powers inperceivable.

They used to be the ceremonies were a beautiful thing. For the magic was freely shared, there was balance. Although some magic was taken, sustenance was always given. And families were given time to heal and recover, and the families were given gifts of land or food. Now a persons blood would be drained into an elder stone. The connection of the souls are now taken over by a machine.

Magic used to be natural. The balance of elements, the balance of people, the balance of the sun and the moon. But now it'd become a beast. It'd become a terrible thing. 

Now it is painful and sometimes results in death on the spot. Afterwards the children were left with nothing, and most never regained any of their magic back. 

During one ceremony, the king drained the blood of an entire village. Taking every last drop leaving only the empty shells of children left.

Children began being tested at birth. And those who showed promise were separated and monitored. Those who didn't show any promise were considered invalids. And were sent to toil in the fields. For a while people stopped having children. But it only resulted in forced impregnation.

King varus's father would only take magic freely shared. And any excess magic was rationed or stored.

He believed that magic was for everyone. And that too much power to any one person would result in corruption and destruction of civilization.

That's why varus killed his father and his brother.

Plot twist; they were still alive but constantly being drained. They were located in tombs within King varus's private chambers.
